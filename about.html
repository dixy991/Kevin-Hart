Who is Kevin Hart?

Kevin Hart exploded onto the scene as one of the most versatile comedic actors in both television and film; and he’s making history.

Hart has appeared in such films as Meet the Fockers sequel aka Little Fockers, and Death at a Funeral, alongside Chris Rock, Tracy Morgan and Martin Lawrence. He also co-starred alongside Matthew McConaughey and Kate Hudson in Fool’s Gold, Along Came Polly opposite Ben Stiller and Jennifer Aniston as well as opposite Steve Carell in The 40 Year Old Virgin.
